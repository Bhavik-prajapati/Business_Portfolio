Who we are

Welcome to Painter Anil Prajapati — a family-run sign and painting workshop located in Himatnagar, Gujarat. We specialize in designing, painting and producing high-quality banners, signboards, radium plates, digital vehicle number plates, stickers and local display boards. With over 15+ years of experience in traditional hand-painting and modern digital printing, we combine craftsmanship with today's production techniques.

Why customers choose us

  • Fast, reliable turnaround for urgent jobs.
  • Combination of hand-painted artistry and digital precision.
  • Affordable pricing for local businesses and vehicle owners.
  • On-site installation and custom design support.

Services we offer

Banners & Posters

Large-format banners for events, shops and hoardings. UV-resistant inks and multiple finishing options (grommets, pole pockets).

Radium / Reflective Plates

High-visibility radium plates for signboards and safety signage — long lasting and visible at night.

Digital Number Plates

Vehicle number plates with clean typography, reflective options and authorised printing standards. Available for cars, bikes and commercial vehicles.

Local Boards & Shop Signages

Custom shop front boards, painted or printed, cut letters, acrylic and metal mounting.

Vehicle Graphics & Stickers

Full or partial vehicle wraps, vinyl stickers and promotional vehicle graphics for branding on the go.

Hand Painting & Lettering

Traditional hand painting for decorative signboards, portrait or murals. Skilled hand lettering to create a unique look.

About the proprietor — Anil Prajapati

Anil is a master painter with decades of experience in the local signage trade. He trained in classical sign-writing and moved into digital printing while keeping traditional methods alive. He personally oversees design, color matching and quality checks for every job.
